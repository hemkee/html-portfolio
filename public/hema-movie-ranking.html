The Best Movies According to Hema

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Lion King

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful scenes and the emotions portrayed by the animals. Also love the story.

URI (Hindi)

Amazing bravery shown by the Indian soldiers. Lights the spark of patriotism and pride in every Indian.

Gandhadha Gudi3 (Kannada)

Super beautiful film. Reveals the hidden beauty of Karnataka. Makes you fall in love with the place.

Body Guard

Super thriller and a very handsome Hero. Beatiful Songs

Sound of Music

A very beautiful Musical
